Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for jobs on ZenithHire?

You can search for jobs by using the search bar on the homepage and applying filters like location, role, or industry.

Can I apply for jobs without uploading a resume?

It is recommended to upload a resume to improve your chances of getting shortlisted, but you can still browse and save jobs without it.

How will I know if a recruiter has viewed my application?

You’ll receive a notification on your dashboard once your application has been viewed by the recruiter.
